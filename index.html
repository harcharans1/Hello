Ang 305 Raag Asa

ਗੁਰਮੁਖਿ ਨਾਮੁ ਮੰਨਿ ਵਸੈ ਸਚੁ ਧਨੁ ਰਾਸਿ ॥

गुरमुखि नामु मंनि वसै सचु धनु रासि ॥

Translation

The Gurmukh enshrines the Naam within the mind; the True Name is the treasure of wealth.

Transliteration

Gurmukh nām man vasai sach dhan rās.
